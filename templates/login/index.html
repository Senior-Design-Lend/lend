About Lend



Lend is a service that allows users to lend out and borrow items from other users. There are many times when a person wants to use some items, but they don't want to fully purchase it. They'll only need it once and then never again. Or maybe someone forgot something at home and needs it for that day. Why buy a new item completely when someone can lend it out to you instead?

Frequently Asked Questions





Is Lend free to use?

Lend is completely free to use!


Is Lend for mobile?

Currently you can only use Lend through the website but there are plans for mobile development in the future.


How do you find items to rent or borrow?

Using our map view, you can search for items in a given area


What if the item I want isn't available?

If it is not available then you can request it! Your request will be listed and you will be notified if someone fulfills it.


How do I contact a Lender?

You can use our built-in chat service to contact a lender.


How do I know if a user is legitimate?

User ratings and reviews will help you determine users you want to make transactions with.
